The IRC ChannelÂ¶

The IRC channel is a great place to talk to other developers that use Panda3D, and to ask questions about anything related to Panda3D.

Network FreeNode
Server chat.freenode.net
Channel #panda3d

You need an IRC client to connect to the channel. If you do not have an IRC client, you can use the web client: http://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=panda3d

Although you will often get a response fairly quickly, depending on the time of day and timezone of our users, it may take longer for people to notice your message and respond. It is recommended that you stay on the channel after asking your question. Asking whether you may ask a question is pointless.
